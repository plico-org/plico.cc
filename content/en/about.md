## Why Plico

Plico is the answer to the question: "_Is there a **simple** app to sign PDF documents?_"

The key to that answer lies in the word "_simple_": in this context, it takes on different shades of meaning depending on who needs to sign a document. _Simple_ means "Easy to use", _Simple_ means "I don't want to create yet another account on yet another online service that fills my inbox with spam", _Simple_ means "Free", _Simple_ means "My data is mine, and I want it to stay that way", _Simple_ means "I don't want to turn on the printer that can't print anyway because it's out of magenta".

Plico is for everyone like us. We want the app to do just two things, but do them well. In short, **a simple, fast way to sign PDFs**.
